Overview

AssetCurve is a local, simple compound-growth simulator for creating rough estimates from your own inputs.

Use Future Value mode to estimate a possible future asset amount from an initial amount, monthly contribution, expected annual return, and number of years. Use Goal Backsolve mode to estimate the monthly contribution that would be needed for a target amount under the same simplified assumptions.
