Overview

PoopTap is a simple poop tracker and bowel movement log for private everyday tracking. It is designed for quick personal records rather than a clinical workflow.

Use it to log bowel movements, stool notes, digestion notes, gut health notes, and constipation notes on a lightweight calendar. PoopTap keeps the experience playful with achievements and app-based progress while staying clear of medical advice.

Who It Is For

  • People who want a simple daily bowel movement tracker
  • Users who want a private stool log or poop tracker
  • People who want lightweight digestion notes, gut health notes, or constipation notes
  • Users who prefer playful personal tracking over a medical-style app

Main Features

  • Quick bowel movement logging
  • Stool notes with optional shape and color context
  • Digestion notes, gut health notes, and constipation notes for personal reference
  • Calendar history for reviewing everyday toilet habits
  • PoopTap Plus optional iCloud Backup & Restore, customization, sharing, and collection features

How to Use

  1. Install PoopTap from the App Store.
  2. Tap after a bowel movement to add a quick log.
  3. Add optional stool notes, digestion notes, gut health notes, or constipation notes when useful.
  4. Review your history on the calendar.
  5. Use PoopTap as a personal log, not as medical advice or analysis.

FAQ

What is PoopTap?

PoopTap is a simple poop tracker and bowel movement log for private everyday tracking.

Is PoopTap a medical app?

No. PoopTap is a simple tracking app for personal notes. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, analysis, or recommendations.

What can I track with PoopTap?

You can log bowel movements, stool notes, digestion notes, gut health notes, constipation notes, and basic toilet habit history for personal reference.

Is PoopTap private?

PoopTap is designed for lightweight personal tracking. Check the App Store product page for Apple's current privacy label and disclosure details before installing.

Does PoopTap analyze stool or give recommendations?

No. PoopTap is for logging and reviewing your own notes. It does not analyze stool, score health, or recommend medical actions.

PoopTap Plus is an optional in-app purchase for features such as iCloud Backup & Restore, extra customization, premium share cards, additional collection features, and more achievements.
