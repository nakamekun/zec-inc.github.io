LaundryTap

A quick tap-based laundry record for remembering whether the laundry was done, moved, or checked.

Overview

LaundryTap is for a common household question: did I do the laundry, move it, or check it? The app keeps the workflow small so recording the status takes less effort than trying to remember it later.

It is useful for people sharing household routines, doing laundry between other tasks, or wanting a lightweight record rather than a full chore management system.

Who It Is For

  • People who forget whether they started or finished laundry
  • Households that want a small status record
  • Users who prefer a dedicated tap tool over a full chore app
  • Busy users doing laundry between work, errands, or family routines

Main Features

  • Quick laundry status recording
  • Simple repeated-use workflow
  • Focused household utility rather than broad task management
  • Useful for shared routines and everyday checks

How to Use

  1. Install LaundryTap from the App Store.
  2. Open it when you start, move, finish, or check laundry.
  3. Record the status in the app.
  4. Return later when you are unsure what happened.
  5. Use it as a lightweight memory aid for the household routine.

FAQ

What is LaundryTap for?

LaundryTap helps you record laundry status so you can quickly check whether the task was done or still needs attention.

Is LaundryTap a chore management app?

No. It is intentionally smaller and focused on laundry status rather than managing every household task.

Who should use LaundryTap?

It is useful for individuals, families, and shared households that often need to remember laundry status.

Does LaundryTap replace reminders?

LaundryTap is mainly a record and check tool. Check the App Store page for the current reminder-related features.

Why use a dedicated laundry app?

A focused app can make a repeated household check faster than opening a large notes or task system.
